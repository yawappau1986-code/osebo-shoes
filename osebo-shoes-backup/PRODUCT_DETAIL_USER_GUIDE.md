# Product Detail Page - User Guide

## 📱 How to Use the Enhanced Product Detail Page

### Opening Product Details
1. Browse the product catalog on the home screen
2. Tap on any product card (either the image or product name)
3. Product detail modal slides up from the bottom

### Viewing Product Images
- **Swipe left/right** on the main image to see all product photos
- **Tap dots** below the image to jump to a specific photo
- **Tap thumbnails** at the bottom to quickly navigate between images
- Images load with smooth animations and loading indicators

### Selecting Product Options

#### 1. Choose Size/Weight (if applicable)
Some products offer multiple size options:
- **US 7** = 250g package
- **US 8** = 500g package  
- **US 9-11** = 1kg package

**How to select:**
- Tap any size button
- Active selection turns red (oxblood color)
- Price updates automatically
- Helper text shows what the size means

#### 2. Choose Quantity
Control how many items to add:

**Increase Quantity:**
- Tap the **[+]** button on the right
- Quantity increases by 1
- Total price updates instantly
- Stock limit prevents over-ordering

**Decrease Quantity:**
- Tap the **[-]** button on the left
- Quantity decreases by 1
- Minimum quantity is 1 (button disabled at 1)

**Current Quantity:**
- Large number displayed in the center
- Shows how many items you're adding

### Viewing Total Price
- Gray box displays the calculated total
- Format: **"Total Price: GHC XX.XX"**
- Updates in real-time as you change:
  - Size/weight selection
  - Quantity amount

### Checking Stock Availability
Look for stock information in two places:
1. **Below product price** - Shows total available stock
2. **Below quantity selector** - Shows "X available"

**Stock Status:**
- ✅ "12 in stock" = Product available
- ❌ "Out of stock" = Cannot purchase

### Sharing Products
Want to share with friends?
1. Tap the **share icon** in the top-right corner
2. Native share menu opens
3. Choose how to share:
   - WhatsApp
   - SMS/Messages
   - Email
   - Facebook
   - Instagram
   - Copy link
   - Any other installed apps

**What gets shared:**
- Product name
- Current price (based on your size selection)
- Full description
- Image URL

### Adding to Cart
Ready to purchase?

1. Review your selections:
   - ✓ Size/weight chosen
   - ✓ Quantity set
   - ✓ Total price confirmed
   
2. Tap **"Add X to Cart • GHC XX.XX"** button
   - Button shows quantity being added
   - Button shows total price
   
3. Product detail closes automatically
4. Items added to your cart
5. Cart icon updates with new item count

**Out of Stock Products:**
- Button turns gray
- Shows "Out of Stock" message
- Button is disabled (cannot tap)
- Alert shown if attempting to purchase

### Closing Product Details
Three ways to close:
1. Tap **back arrow** (top-left)
2. Swipe down on the modal (iOS/Android gesture)
3. Tap outside the modal area
4. After adding to cart (auto-closes)

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Compare Prices
- Switch between sizes to see bulk discounts
- Larger sizes often have better per-gram pricing

### Tip 2: Bulk Ordering
- Use quantity selector for multiple items
- Saves time vs. adding one at a time
- See total cost before adding to cart

### Tip 3: Check Stock
- Stock count shows before adding to cart
- Low stock items (<5) should be ordered soon
- Out of stock items can't be purchased

### Tip 4: Share & Save
- Share product links to your notes app
- Send to yourself via email for later
- Share with family to coordinate orders

### Tip 5: Image Gallery
- View all product angles before buying
- Zoom by tapping (if supported)
- Check packaging, labels, certifications

## 🎯 Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Buying Multiple Products
**Goal:** Add 3 units of 1kg size to cart

1. Open product detail
2. Select "US 9" (1kg option)
3. Tap [+] button twice (quantity = 3)
4. Verify total: "Total Price: GHC XX.XX"
5. Tap "Add 3 to Cart"

### Scenario 2: Comparing Sizes
**Goal:** Find best value for money

1. Open product detail
2. Tap "US 7" - note price (250g)
3. Tap "US 8" - note price (500g)
4. Tap "US 9" - note price (1kg)
5. Calculate per-gram cost
6. Select best value
7. Add to cart

### Scenario 3: Sharing with Family
**Goal:** Ask family member which size they want

1. Open product detail
2. Tap share icon (top-right)
3. Choose WhatsApp
4. Select family member
5. Message sent with product details
6. They reply with preferred size
7. Return to app, select size, add to cart

### Scenario 4: Low Stock Alert
**Goal:** Order before product runs out

1. Open product detail
2. See "2 in stock" warning
3. Set quantity to 2 (maximum available)
4. Add to cart immediately
5. Proceed to checkout to secure order

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- **Stock limits** prevent ordering more than available
- **Minimum quantity** is always 1 (can't add 0)
- **Price updates** automatically based on size selection
- **Total price** includes quantity calculation
- **Share feature** uses device's native sharing
- **Out of stock** products cannot be added to cart

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Can't increase quantity  
**Solution:** Check stock availability - you may have reached the limit

**Problem:** Share button not working  
**Solution:** Ensure your device has sharing enabled in settings

**Problem:** Images not loading  
**Solution:** Check internet connection, try closing and reopening

**Problem:** Price shows wrong amount  
**Solution:** Verify size selection - different sizes have different prices

**Problem:** Can't add to cart  
**Solution:** Check if product is out of stock or quantity is set to 0
